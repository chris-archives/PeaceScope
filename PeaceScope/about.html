ABOUT PEACESCOPE

PeaceScope is a local platform founded by Christian Lin and Jah'Mosi Joslyn. At PeaceScope, we are driven by a shared vision: a world where every community thrives without the shadow of gun violence. Our mission is to create lasting impact through grassroots-driven initiatives, fostering connections, and empowering change. Scroll left to read more →

OUR PURPOSE

PeaceScope was born out of necessity—a response to the urgent need for action against gun violence. We recognize that underserved communities in Philadelphia face disproportionate challenges, and we are committed to making a difference. Our purpose is clear: to build a safer, more resilient city by addressing the root causes of violence.

OUR APPROACH

Community-Centric Solutions:

We believe in the power of local knowledge and collaboration. Our approach centers around working side by side with community members. Together, we prioritize projects that address immediate needs and create sustainable change.

Education and Awareness:

Through workshops, seminars, and community events, we raise awareness about gun violence prevention. We empower individuals with knowledge, equipping them to be advocates for peace within their neighborhoods.

Advocacy and Policy Reform:

Peacescope actively engages with policymakers, advocating for evidence-based policies that reduce gun violence. We believe in systemic change and work towards creating a safer environment for all.

OUR IMPACT

Our work extends beyond statistics. It’s about transforming lives for generations. By fostering understanding, promoting dialogue, and providing resources, we aim to break the cycle of violence. Together, we can create a Philadelphia where every child grows up without fear and every family thrives.
